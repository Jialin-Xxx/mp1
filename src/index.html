About Beijing WB

Beijing WB is a professional Honor of Kings (KPL) esports team based in Beijing, China. The team was formerly known as TS, a squad that rapidly rose to fame by winning the 2020 KPL Spring Championship and the 2020 Honor of Kings World Champion Cup. In 2021, the club was officially acquired and rebranded as Beijing WB (Weibo Gaming). Since its rebranding, Beijing WB has remained one of the most competitive teams in the league, known for its tactical discipline, youthful roster, and thrilling playstyle. Combining the legacy of TS with the resources of Weibo Gaming, Beijing WB continues to strive for new championships while inspiring millions of Honor of Kings fans worldwide.
